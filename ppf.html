Frequently Asked Questions

How much does paint protection film cost?

PPF pricing varies by coverage area. A full-front package (hood, fenders, bumper, mirrors) typically ranges from $1,500 to $2,500. Full-body PPF ranges from $5,000 to $8,000+ depending on the vehicle. We use exclusively XPEL films with a 10-year warranty.

Does PPF damage paint when removed?

No. When professionally installed and removed, XPEL paint protection film will not damage your factory paint. In fact, the paint underneath is often in better condition than unprotected areas since PPF shields against UV, chemicals, and physical damage.

How long does PPF last?

XPEL Ultimate Plus and Stealth films are backed by a 10-year manufacturer warranty against yellowing, cracking, bubbling, and peeling. With proper care, PPF can last the lifetime of your vehicle ownership.

What is self-healing PPF?

Self-healing PPF contains an elastomeric polymer top coat that automatically repairs light scratches and swirl marks when exposed to heat — either from the sun or warm water. This keeps your film looking flawless without any maintenance.

Can you put PPF over a vinyl wrap?

Yes, although it's generally better to apply PPF directly to the paint first, then wrap over it if desired. We can advise on the best approach for your specific project during a consultation.
