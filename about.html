About Us

Our Story

Built by car enthusiasts, for car enthusiasts.

Built on Passion, Driven by Quality

Haus of Wraps is San Diego's trusted destination for premium vehicle customization. Founded by Jon, an ASE-certified technician and lifelong car enthusiast, we bring the same passion to every project — whether it's a weekend cruiser or a six-figure exotic.

Every install starts with proper prep: exterior detailing, careful bumper and trim removal, precision installation, and quality reassembly. No shortcuts, no rushing — just the kind of work we'd want done on our own cars.

With over 2,500 vehicles wrapped and a perfect 5-star Google rating, our reputation speaks for itself. We're proud to be trusted by San Diego's most discerning car owners.
