Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to wrap a Tesla?

Tesla wrap pricing depends on the model and coverage. A full color change wrap for a Model 3 or Model Y typically starts around $3,000–$5,000. Model S and Model X may cost more due to their larger surface area. We offer free estimates for every Tesla model.

Does Tesla paint need PPF?

Tesla uses single-stage paint that is thinner and softer than most manufacturers. This makes it more susceptible to rock chips, scratches, and swirl marks. We strongly recommend at minimum a full-front PPF package using XPEL Ultimate Plus to protect your investment.

What is a Tesla chrome delete?

A chrome delete replaces the shiny chrome trim pieces on your Tesla with a satin or gloss black vinyl wrap. This gives your Tesla a sleeker, more modern look and is one of our most popular Tesla modifications.

Can you wrap a Tesla with autopilot sensors?

Yes. Our installers are experienced with Tesla's cameras, sensors, and autopilot hardware. We carefully work around all sensors and ensure full functionality after installation.
