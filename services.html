Our Services

Everything your vehicle needs under one roof in San Diego.

🎨

Color Change Wraps

Full-body vinyl wraps in matte, satin, gloss, chrome, and custom finishes. We use premium materials from 3M, Avery Dennison, and Hexis. Every install includes bumper removal, surface prep, and precision application for a paint-like finish.

🛡

Paint Protection Film

Self-healing clear bra using XPEL Ultimate Plus and Stealth. Full front, full body, or custom coverage to protect your paint from rock chips, scratches, and road debris. 10-year manufacturer warranty included.

☀

Ceramic Window Tint

Premium ceramic window tint that blocks 99% of UV rays and up to 60% of solar heat. Reduce glare, protect your interior, and enhance privacy. Available for all vehicle types including Tesla glass roofs.

⚙

Powder Coating

Custom wheel and caliper finishes in any color. Powder coat is more durable than paint, resists chips, scratches, and brake dust. We strip, prep, coat, and clear for a factory-quality finish.

🚗

Body Kits

Front lips, side skirts, rear diffusers, and spoilers from top manufacturers. Professional test-fitment, prep, paint or wrap matching, and installation for a seamless factory-finished look.

✨

Custom Graphics

Partial wraps, racing stripes, commercial fleet graphics, and custom designs. From concept to installation, we bring your vision to life with precision-cut vinyl and expert application.
